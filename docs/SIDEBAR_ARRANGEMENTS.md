# Sidebar Arrangement Proposals

This document presents three alternative sidebar arrangements for KMainCMS, each maintaining 10 sidebar items but with different grouping strategies and tab reorganizations.

---

## Comparison Summary

| Aspect | Version 1 | Version 2 | Version 3 |
|--------|-----------|-----------|-----------|
| **Naming** | Functional | Church-specific | Journey-based |
| **Learning Curve** | Low | Medium | Low |
| **Church Context** | Low | High | Medium |
| **User Flow** | Task-oriented | Role-oriented | Journey-oriented |
| **Best For** | General users | Church staff | New users |

## Recommendation

**Version 1 (Functional Grouping)** is recommended because:
- Most intuitive for users of all backgrounds
- Clear functional separation makes features easy to find
- Scalable as new features are added
- Lower learning curve for new administrators
- Professional and clean terminology

**Version 2** is best if the primary users are long-time church staff who prefer church terminology.

**Version 3** is best if onboarding new administrators frequently and want to guide them through typical workflows.
