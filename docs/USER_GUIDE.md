## Getting Started

### First Time Login

1. **Access the System**
   - Open your web browser
   - Navigate to your church's KMainCMS URL
   - Example: `https://yourchurch.kmaincms.com`

2. **Log In**
   - Enter your email address
   - Enter your password
   - Click "Sign In"

3. **First-Time Setup**
   - Update your profile information
   - Set your preferences
   - Complete the onboarding checklist

### System Requirements

- Modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
- Internet connection
- Email address for notifications

### Mobile Access

KMainCMS is fully responsive and works on:
- Smartphones (iOS and Android)
- Tablets
- Desktop computers

## Member Management

### Viewing Members

1. Navigate to "Members" from the main menu
2. Browse the member list
3. Use search to find specific members
4. Filter by membership status, department, or group

### Adding a New Member

1. Click "Add Member" button
2. Fill in the required information:
   - Personal details (name, contact info)
   - Membership information
   - Department assignments
   - Group assignments
3. Click "Save"

### Member Profile

Each member has a comprehensive profile including:
- Personal information
- Contact details
- Membership history
- Giving history
- Event participation
- Group memberships
- Notes and follow-ups

### Editing Member Information

1. Go to the member's profile
2. Click "Edit Profile"
3. Update the information
4. Click "Save Changes"

### Member Communication

1. Go to member's profile
2. Click "Send Message"
3. Choose communication method:
   - Email
   - SMS
   - In-app notification
4. Compose your message
5. Click "Send"

### Member Status

Members can have different statuses:
- **Active** - Regularly attending members
- **Inactive** - Not recently active
- **Visitor** - First-time visitors
- **Prospect** - Interested in joining

## Events & Activities

### Viewing Events

1. Navigate to "Events" from the main menu
2. View events in:
   - Calendar view
   - List view
   - Upcoming/Past tabs

### Creating an Event

1. Click "Create Event"
2. Fill in event details:
   - Event name and description
   - Date and time
   - Location
   - Event type (worship, meeting, social, etc.)
   - Maximum attendees (if limited)
3. Add event details:
   - Agenda/items
   - Requirements (what to bring)
   - Contact person
4. Configure registration:
   - Open registration
   - Registration deadline
   - Registration limits
5. Click "Publish Event"

### Event Registration

1. Find the event you want to attend
2. Click "Register"
3. Confirm your registration
4. You'll receive a confirmation

### Managing Your Registrations

1. Go to "My Events" in your profile
2. View your upcoming events
3. Cancel registration if needed (before deadline)

### Event Check-In

For event organizers:
1. Open the event
2. Click "Check-In"
3. Search for member
4. Mark as checked in

## Announcements

### Viewing Announcements

1. Navigate to "Announcements"
2. Browse recent announcements
3. Filter by category or date

### Creating Announcements (Admins)

1. Click "Create Announcement"
2. Fill in details:
   - Title
   - Content
   - Category (general, urgent, event, etc.)
   - Target audience (all, specific groups)
   - Publish date
3. Choose delivery method:
   - In-app only
   - Email
   - SMS
   - All channels
4. Click "Send"

### Announcement Categories

- **General** - Regular updates
- **Urgent** - Important time-sensitive information
- **Event** - Event-related announcements
- **Financial** - Giving and financial updates
- **Volunteer** - Volunteer opportunities

## Giving & Donations

### Making a Donation

1. Navigate to "Giving"
2. Click "Make a Donation"
3. Choose donation type:
   - Tithe
   - Offering
   - Special project
   - Building fund
4. Enter amount
5. Choose payment method:
   - M-Pesa (Kenya)
   - Credit/Debit card
   - Bank transfer
6. Complete payment
7. Receive confirmation

### Giving History

1. Go to "My Giving"
2. View your donation history
3. Filter by date or type
4. Download giving statements

### Recurring Giving

1. Navigate to "Giving"
2. Click "Setup Recurring Giving"
3. Choose frequency:
   - Weekly
   - Bi-weekly
   - Monthly
4. Set amount and payment method
5. Confirm setup

### Donation Receipts

Automatic receipts are sent for all donations:
- Email receipt immediately
- Annual giving statement available in January

## Best Practices

### Security

- **Use strong passwords** - Mix of letters, numbers, and symbols
- **Don't share credentials** - Keep your login information private
- **Log out when done** - Especially on shared devices
- **Report suspicious activity** - Contact admin if you notice anything unusual

### Data Privacy

- **Respect member privacy** - Only access information you need
- **Follow data policies** - Adhere to church data handling guidelines
- **Keep information confidential** - Don't share sensitive data

### Communication

- **Be clear and concise** - Make messages easy to understand
- **Use appropriate channels** - Email for formal, SMS for urgent
- **Respect preferences** - Honor communication preferences of members
- **Proofread before sending** - Check for errors in announcements

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

**Can't log in**
- Check your email and password
- Reset your password if needed
- Clear browser cache
- Try a different browser

**Not receiving notifications**
- Check notification preferences
- Verify email/phone number is correct
- Check spam/junk folders
- Contact admin if issue persists

**Can't find what you're looking for**
- Use the search function
- Check different categories
- Contact support for help

**Payment failed**
- Verify payment details
- Check internet connection
- Try alternative payment method
- Contact treasurer if issue continues
